Personal Overview


My interests are diverse and shift often, and I love learning/applying diverse topics all across engineering, physics, mathematics, and much more.
I can typically be found up late printing parts with my Prusa MK4S 3D Printer, running high-voltage transformer experiments, assembling a stepper motor driver, or researching new material science concepts.
Everything in our universe fascinates me, and I believe the ability to understand how something works is an infinitely valuable privilege.


Outside of engineering, I've played musical instruments for most of my life so when I’m not designing and building I love to play the guitar, drums, and keyboard. I also have a sizable collection of tools, so I routinely help whoever needs it with repairs, installations, and other DIY tasks.

I am always interested in professional opportunities and connecting with like-minded individuals. If you'd like to work together please reach out!
